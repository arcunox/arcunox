Privacy

Arcunox is designed to avoid collecting, storing, or transmitting personal data. Alias generation is deterministic, local, and stateless. This page describes what Arcunox does and does not do.

No Data Collection

Arcunox does not collect, store, log, or transmit any of the following:

  • Emails, seeds, or alias inputs
  • Generated aliases
  • IP addresses or device information
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data
  • Cookies, tracking pixels, or session identifiers

Local-Only Computation

All alias generation happens entirely in your browser. No requests are sent to a server for computation, storage, or analysis.

No Accounts, No Sync, No Telemetry

Arcunox does not use accounts, authentication, cloud storage, or telemetry systems. There is no backend database and no server-side state.

Third-Party Services

Arcunox does not embed third-party scripts, analytics, fonts, or trackers. No external services receive data from your usage.

Security Notes

Because Arcunox stores nothing, there is no stored data to breach or leak. Alias generation logic is inspectable and deterministic.

Contact

For questions or issues, contact the maintainer via the project repository.
