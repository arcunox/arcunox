Privacy

Arcunox is designed to collect nothing, store nothing, and transmit nothing. Every tool runs entirely in your browser. No data leaves your device.

What Arcunox Does Not Do

  • No tracking.
  • No analytics.
  • No cookies.
  • No accounts.
  • No telemetry.
  • No logging.
  • No data retention.
  • No network requests.

Arcunox does not store, profile, fingerprint, or monitor you in any way.

How Tools Work

All processing happens locally in your browser using client-side code. Inputs, outputs, and intermediate data remain on your device and are never sent anywhere.

Closing the page clears everything. Arcunox keeps no history.

Server-Side Tools

If Arcunox introduces optional server-side utilities in the future, they will follow the same principles:

  • No accounts.
  • No logging or analytics.
  • No data retention.
  • No storage of inputs or outputs.
  • Processing only — no record keeping.

All requests are deleted immediately after processing.
