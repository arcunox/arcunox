Arcunox Alias Generator

A monolithic identity engine for deterministic, privacy-preserving email aliases. Inspectable logic. No hidden state. No sentiment. Generates clean, reusable email alias surfaces for controlled, long-term identity workflows.

How it works
  • Input: Provide a seed — an email, domain, context phrase, or any custom string. This seed defines the identity boundary you want to create. Reuse it for consistency, or switch it to segment new surfaces.
  • Transform: Arcunox runs the seed through deterministic, fully inspectable logic to produce an email-ready alias. No accounts, no tracking, no analytics, no behavioural profiling — just clean computation designed for privacy workflows.
  • Output: You receive a stable, reusable email alias you can rotate, revoke, or dedicate to specific services. No metadata leakage, no pattern exposure, and no randomization that breaks long-term workflows — just a controlled identity surface you own.
