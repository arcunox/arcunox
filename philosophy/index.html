Philosophy

Arcunox exists to provide tools that are simple, predictable, and respectful. No noise. No surveillance. No friction. Just clarity and control.

Principles

  • Determinism — tools behave the same today and years from now.
  • Privacy — nothing is tracked, stored, logged, or transmitted.
  • Minimalism — no bloat, no dependencies, no unnecessary features.
  • Local execution — everything runs in your browser.
  • Transparency — simple code, simple structure, no hidden behaviour.
  • Longevity — tools built to last, not to churn.

Intent

Arcunox is not a platform, a service, or a product. It is a quiet space on the internet — a set of tools that do one thing well, without asking for anything in return.

The philosophy may evolve, but the foundation remains: respect the user, respect their time, respect their privacy.
